The Doctor Behind the Clinic

Dr. Puja Prasad

Dr. Puja Prasad is a Senior Consultant in Obstetrics, Gynaecology, and Laparoscopic Surgery with 23+ years of overall clinical experience and 10 years as a specialist. She holds an MBBS and MS in Obstetrics & Gynaecology, along with the DMAS and FMAS fellowships in Minimally Invasive and Minimal Access Surgery.

She has extensive clinical experience working in leading hospitals across Delhi NCR, managing pregnancies including high-risk antenatal care, and safely delivering numerous complex cases. She is also highly skilled in counselling and managing infertile couples, with excellent results. As an expert in gynaecological disorders of adolescence, PCOD, safe MTP, family planning, menopause, and post-menopausal bleeding, she is also a proficient pre-conceptional counsellor.
